Support

Effective date: 14 August 2025

Need help fast? Email wordmergegame@gmail.com. Please include your device model, iOS version, and the WordMerge app version.

Quick Help (FAQ)

The app won’t open or crashes
  • Power cycle your device, then try again.
  • Ensure you’re on the latest iOS version supported by the app (see App Store listing).
  • Delete and reinstall WordMerge (your game has no cloud saves or accounts, so you won’t lose personal data).
  • Still crashing? Email us with steps to reproduce, screenshots/video if possible, and crash time.
I found a bug—how do I report it?
  • Describe exactly what you did and what happened instead.
  • Include: device model (e.g., iPhone 14), iOS version, WordMerge app version (see App Store listing).
  • Email: wordmergegame@gmail.com
Do you collect any data?

No. WordMerge does not collect, store, or share personal data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Troubleshooting Checklist

  • Update to the latest version of WordMerge from the App Store.
  • Free up some storage space and try again.
  • Disable Low Power Mode temporarily if performance seems reduced.
  • Restart your device.
